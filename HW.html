About

Cockatoos are intelligent and social parrots known for their playful nature and striking crests. Native to Australia and nearby islands, these birds form strong bonds with their flock and human caretakers, click here to learn more about them

Conservation

Cockatoos face increasing threats due to habitat loss, illegal pet trade, and climate change. Conservation efforts focus on protecting their natural environment, enforcing wildlife laws, and raising awareness to ensure these intelligent birds thrive for generations to come.

Gallery

Explore the stunning world of cockatoos through our gallery! From vibrant crests to playful interactions, these images capture the beauty, intelligence, and personality of these incredible birds.

Thinking About a Pet Cockatoo?

Cockatoos are affectionate and intelligent, but they require lifelong commitment, social interaction, and proper care. Before bringing one home, learn about their needs, potential challenges, and daily responsibilities. Click the link to discover if a cockatoo is the right pet for you!
